WHAT IS A 'BODEGA'?

In Peru, independent small grocery stores are called ‘bodegas’.
There are 2+ million stores only in Latin America and they are considered a natural solution for unemployment.

Women Empowerment

70% of bodegas are owned and managed by women.

Social Inclusion

It is where low-income families buy groceries on a daily basis.

Economic Development

They provide daily sustenance for 10+ million people only in Latin America.

Economic Relevance

They provide 1/3 of their country's GDP.


Challenge

CHALLENGE

Bodegas and convenience stores are not playing in the same level field.



Our Solution

OUR SOLUTION

Turbodega is a software management tool that builds a network of small grocers in emerging countries and tracks their daily-sales in real time.



How it Works

HOW IT WORKS

Social Mission

SOCIAL MISSION
